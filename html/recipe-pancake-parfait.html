Pancake with Greek Yogurt Berry Parfait

Layers of pancakes, Greek yogurt, and berries for a light sweet treat.

⏱ 25–30 min 🍽 1–2 servings 🥞 Breakfast · Sweets · High Protein

Ingredients

  • Small pancakes (homemade or ready)
  • Greek yogurt
  • Mixed berries (fresh or frozen)
  • Honey or maple syrup
  • Optional: nuts or granola for crunch

Steps

  1. Prepare small pancakes and let them cool slightly.
  2. In a glass or bowl, add a layer of pancake pieces.
  3. Add a layer of Greek yogurt on top.
  4. Add berries and drizzle honey or maple syrup.
  5. Repeat the layers and finish with berries and nuts on top.
