Dark Chocolate Almond Bites

Healthy dark chocolate treats for a low-carb sweet snack.

⏱ 15–20 min 🍽 8–10 bites 🍫 Sweets · Low Carb

Ingredients

  • Dark chocolate (70%+ cacao)
  • Almonds or mixed nuts
  • Optional: coconut flakes
  • Optional: pinch of sea salt

Steps

  1. Melt the dark chocolate gently (microwave or double boiler).
  2. Place nuts in small clusters on baking paper.
  3. Pour melted chocolate over each cluster to cover the nuts.
  4. Sprinkle with coconut flakes or sea salt if desired.
  5. Let them set in the fridge until firm, then serve.
