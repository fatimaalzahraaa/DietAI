Fried Eggs

Simple fried eggs, perfect for a quick high-protein breakfast.

⏱ 5–8 min 🍽 1 serving 🍳 Breakfast · High Protein

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp olive oil or butter
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Optional: paprika, herbs, toast on the side

Steps

  1. Heat a small pan over medium heat and add oil or butter.
  2. Crack the eggs gently into the pan.
  3. Cook until the whites are set and the yolks are at your preferred doneness.
  4. Season with salt, pepper, and optional paprika or herbs.
  5. Serve immediately, optionally with toast or veggies on the side.
