Herb Chicken with Baby Potatoes

Juicy herb-marinated chicken served with roasted potatoes and sautéed green beans.

⏱ 35–40 min 🍽 2 servings 💪 Lunch · High Protein

Ingredients

  • 2 small chicken breasts
  • 1 cup baby potatoes, cut in halves
  • 1 cup green beans
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt, pepper, mixed dried herbs (rosemary, thyme, oregano)

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and season the potatoes with olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper.
  2. Bake the potatoes until golden and crispy on the outside.
  3. Marinate the chicken with olive oil, herbs, salt, and pepper, then cook it in a pan or oven.
  4. Sauté the green beans with a little oil, salt, and pepper until tender.
  5. Plate the chicken with the potatoes and beans and serve warm.
