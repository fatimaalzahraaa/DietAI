Golden Baby Potatoes

Soft and crispy roasted baby potatoes seasoned with herbs.

⏱ 30–35 min 🍽 2–3 servings 🥔 Lunch / Side Dish

Ingredients

  • 2 cups baby potatoes
  • 1–2 tbsp olive oil
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Garlic (fresh or powder)
  • Fresh or dried rosemary

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and line a tray with baking paper.
  2. Wash and dry the baby potatoes, cut larger ones in half.
  3. Toss potatoes with olive oil, salt, pepper, garlic, and rosemary.
  4. Spread in a single layer and bake until golden and crispy.
  5. Serve hot as a side or snack.
