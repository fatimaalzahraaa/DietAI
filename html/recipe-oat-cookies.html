Oatmeal Banana and Chocolate Cookies

Soft cookies made with oats, banana and chocolate, naturally sweet.

⏱ 20–25 min 🍽 8–10 cookies 🍪 Breakfast · Sweets

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe banana, mashed
  • 1 cup oats
  • Chocolate chips or chopped chocolate
  • Optional: cinnamon
  • Optional: nuts or seeds

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven and line a baking tray.
  2. Mash the banana in a bowl.
  3. Add oats and mix until combined.
  4. Stir in chocolate and any optional ingredients.
  5. Form small cookies on the tray and bake until set and lightly golden.
