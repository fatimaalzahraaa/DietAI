Healthy Beans For Lunch

High protein, keto-friendly beans dish for a filling lunch.

⏱ 20–25 min 🍽 2 servings 💪 Lunch · High Protein

Ingredients

  • 2 cups cooked beans (any type you like)
  • 1 tbsp olive oil
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • Garlic, chopped or crushed
  • Salt, pepper, and preferred spices

Steps

  1. Heat olive oil in a pan and sauté onion until soft.
  2. Add garlic and cook briefly until fragrant.
  3. Add cooked beans, season with salt, pepper, and spices.
  4. Let it simmer a few minutes for flavors to mix.
  5. Serve warm, optionally with a side salad or veggies.
