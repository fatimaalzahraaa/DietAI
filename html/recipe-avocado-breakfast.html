Fresh Avocado For Breakfast

A nutrient-rich avocado loaded with healthy fats and vitamins.

⏱ 5–10 min 🍽 1 serving 🥑 Breakfast · Low Carb

Ingredients

  • 1 ripe avocado
  • 1–2 slices whole grain toast (optional)
  • Salt and black pepper
  • Lemon juice
  • Optional: chili flakes, olive oil, seeds (chia, sesame)

Steps

  1. Cut the avocado in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Mash lightly with a fork, leaving some chunks.
  3. Add salt, pepper, and a squeeze of lemon juice.
  4. Serve on toast or in the avocado skin as a bowl.
  5. Top with chili flakes or seeds if you like extra crunch.
